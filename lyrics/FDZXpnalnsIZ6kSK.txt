Rise slowly
From Mother Earth's arms
While your golden lights
Let me drown in an ocean of beauty.

Taken away
From this warm and everlasting white
And thrown into
A world of impermanence and hate.

Striving to find a home,
A purpose
Until I become part of you,
Part of you.

Illuminate...
Illuminate...
